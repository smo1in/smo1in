Who are we?

Wise Recruitment was founded and is led by Alexandra Wise, who has a deep understanding of the hospitality industry and a wealth of experience working in the field. Her own journey in hospitality began with a job abroad facilitated by an agency and now with over a decade of experience, including a managerial position in Saudi Arabia.

Trusted in talent search

As specialists in retained searches, we have a proven track record of successfully identifying and placing top executive and non-executive talent across the hospitality sector. From international hotel chains to small independent companies, we have experience in every function within the industry.

Working together

At the core of every hospitality business are the people who work there. As such, our priority is to fully understand our clients’ staffing needs and requirements. Based on this understanding, we work in partnership with our clients to create a detailed, bespoke brief that outlines their staffing needs, whether they are seeking a single senior leader or an entire team.
