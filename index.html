Who are we?

«Wise recruitment» was founded and leading by Alexandra Wise, who spent over 10 years working in hospitality and explored the world from Europe to Asia.
Alexandra started her career as a waitress, grew up to a professional with a strong understanding of hospitality business and demands of the market.
Currently Alexandra holds a managerial position in Saudi Arabia, as a valuable part of a successful company.

Trusted in talent search

We are specialists in retained searches for executive and non-executive talent across the sector, from international hotel groups to small independent companies.
With experience in every function within the hospitality industry, we have an excellent track record and trusted reputation based on mutual understanding, flexibility, trust, and integrity.

Working together

People are at the heart of every hospitality business, so our priority is to understand exactly who and what you need.
We will work in partnership with you to create an entirely bespoke brief, whether you need a senior leader or a whole new team.
We will spend time learning about your business from every angle so that we can fully appreciate the type of person, expertise, and approach you will need to achieve your goals. Together we will create a detailed brief that fully describes your requirements.
